Join Us

Are you an MIT student interested in joining Lion Dance? Everyone is welcome!

  • We're very beginner friendly (no experience necessary)
  • Practices once to twice a week
  • Perform in cool places around the Boston area
  • A close, tight-knit community

You can email us at lion-dance-exec@mit.edu to get more information about the club and about when practices are!
